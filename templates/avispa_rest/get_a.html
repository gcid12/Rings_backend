What is a Ring?

Answer:

A Ring is a Data repository that lives in the cloud and/or locally in connected devices. What makes Ring different from other repositories or databases is that it connects with a Global Ring where all the data is merged to create Super Datasets.

How it works:

Rich Data sets allows apps or A.I. systems to digg into more complex and granular queries than the regular 'Scrapping' approach used nowadays.
